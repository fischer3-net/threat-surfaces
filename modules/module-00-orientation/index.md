# Module 00 — Orientation & Prerequisites

**Purpose**: Calibration module. Establish notation, verify the starting point, and surface any gaps before they become obstacles.

## Units

| Unit | Title | Est. Time |
|---|---|---|
| [Unit 01](unit-01-vectors-and-vector-spaces/notes.md) | Vectors & Vector Spaces | 50–60 min |
| [Unit 02](unit-02-dot-and-cross-products/notes.md) | Dot Products & Cross Products | 55–65 min |
| [Unit 03](unit-03-single-variable-review/notes.md) | Single-Variable Review & Notation Setup | 45–55 min |

## Module Learning Objectives

- Confirm fluency with vectors and vector operations in ℝ² and ℝ³
- Establish the notation conventions used throughout this course
- Identify and address any gaps in single-variable calculus fluency
